About OptiMann

We're a team dedicated to transforming mental health care. Our mission is simple: equip people with the tools and support they need to thrive—not just cope, but truly flourish.

Holistic Mission

At OptiMann, we recognize that mental health extends far beyond symptom management—it's about fostering a balanced connection between mind, body, and spirit. We collaborate to craft tailored, personalized care plans for every individual we serve.

Our Vision

Inspiring a global transformation, OptiMann envisions a future where individuals effortlessly prioritize their emotional well-being through innovative technology. A world where everyone possesses the means to comprehend, nurture, and elevate their emotional wellness.

Our Services

Comprehensive suite of services designed to enrich your mind.

Wellness Screening

An online platform that offers users the ability to assess their Emotional Wellness Index with personalized insights.

Virtual Teletherapy

Sessions by licensed professionals. Individual, group, and specialized modalities like CBT and DBT.

Wellness Workshops

Webinars on stress management, resilience-building, and coping strategies.

Mindfulness Programs

Meditation programs tailored to beginners and experienced practitioners through experts.

Our Approach

Founded by Dr. Sandeep Vohra & Kapil Kumria, OptiMann pioneers mental health care with a holistic approach, integrating the latest advancements in psychiatry, therapy, and wellness coaching. Our mission is to revolutionize mental health by providing personalized care plans.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025

OptiMann is committed to the principle of fair and ethical business practices and ensuring the privacy of records and related information for its clients (e.g., employees and associated staff) who utilize the full spectrum of psychiatric/medical and allied services provided by OptiMann.

Confidentiality Responsibility

It is OptiMann's legal and ethical responsibility to protect the privacy, confidentiality of records, proprietary information, and other confidential information relating to its clients.

Data Access & Security

Under no circumstances will anyone, including Corporate Heads of the corporation, HR Departments, Authorised Signatories, or business partners (with whom OptiMann might tie up), have access to client's data stored with OptiMann, unless mandated by a court of law.

We do not disclose client data to anyone. This includes (but is not limited to) a client's physical and mental health records, diagnostic and therapeutic reports, psychological analysis, and inputs shared with the OptiMann team.
